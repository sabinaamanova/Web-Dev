Skillet Zucchini and Squash

Step 1: Heat butter and olive oil together in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion once butter is bubbly; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add bell pepper and cook for 1 minute. Stir in yellow squash and zucchini slices. Season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, for about 3 minutes.
Step 2 Stir in minced garlic and cook, stirring, about 1 minute. Keep cooking, stirring continuously, until squash has desired texture, 2 to 3 minutes.
Step 3 Sprinkle with minced chives before serving.

Recipe #2
Macaroni and Cheese

  • Time:
    • Prep time Cook time Total time
    10 min 15 min 25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
macaroni
butter
all-purpose flour
salt
milk
shredded Cheddar cheese
 1 (8 ounce) box
¼ cup
¼ cup
½ teaspoon
2 cups
2 cups

Step 1: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
Step 2 At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
Step 3 Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
Step 4 Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.
Step 5 Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.
Step 6 Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.

Recipe #3
Pizza

  • Time:
    • Prep time Cook time Total time
    25 min 15 min 40 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
warm water
active dry yeast
white sugar
bread flour
olive oil
salt		 1 cup
1 (.25 ounce) package
1 teaspoon
2 ½ cups
2 tablespoons
1 teaspoon

Step 1: Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a pizza pan.
Step 2 Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
Step 3 Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier. Let rest for 5 minutes.
Step 4Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
Step 5 Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
Step 6 Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
