The coolest creature

Parrots, the most famous exotic birds, which from time immemorial people began to keep in homes, not for profit, meat or eggs, but simply as entertainment, so to speak, for the soul.

Species

There are more than three hundred different species of parrots in the world, and all of them live in countries with humid tropical climates.
Among the multitude of parrots there are several species that are capable of copying overheard sounds, including bird voices and human speech.
